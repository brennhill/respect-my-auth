# Compliance Readiness Checklist

Date: 2026-02-15

## SOC2 Readiness
- Access control policy documented
- MFA enforced for admin access
- RBAC roles defined and reviewed
- Audit logs retained and immutable
- Change management process documented
- Incident response runbooks complete
- Monitoring and alerting in place
- Vendor risk assessments completed

## GDPR Readiness
- Data processing agreements (DPA) templates
- Right-to-access export flow
- Right-to-erasure flow
- Data retention policy documented
- EU data residency option
- Privacy policy and data mapping complete

## Owners and Timelines
- Security Lead: access control, incident response
- Engineering: logging, monitoring, deletion workflows
- Legal/Compliance: DPA templates, privacy policy

## Evidence Artifacts
- Access reviews
- Deployment logs
- Incident reports
- Audit log exports
